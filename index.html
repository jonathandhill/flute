About Sandrine

Flautist Sandrine Jones is currently a postgraduate student studying with Sam Coles and Karen Jones at the Royal Academy of Music.

Previously, Sandrine studied in Durham and Cambridge where was principal flute of both Durham University Palatinate Orchestra and CUMS Symphony Orchestra.

In London, Sandrine is enjoying a varied career as a freelance musician, collaborating in orchestral and chamber projects. A keen teacher, Sandrine also teaches flute at James Allen's Girls' School, Notre Dame School and James Allen's Saturday School for the Performing Arts.
